End of Lease Cleaning Services

Ensure a smooth transition with our End of Lease Cleaning at Crystal Clean Sydney. We provide a detailed clean that guarantees the return of your security deposit and leaves the property in pristine condition for its next occupants.

What We Offer:

  • Full-Property Cleaning: We perform all standard general cleaning tasks plus deep cleaning of all rooms, including kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Appliance Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of appliances like ovens and refrigerators to leave them looking as good as new.
  • Detailed Attention: Special focus on light fixtures, inside cabinets, and closets, ensuring every corner meets the highest standards.
  • Window Washing and Blind Cleaning: Includes cleaning of all windows inside and out (where accessible), providing a clear view and enhancing natural light, along with thorough cleaning of all blinds.
  • Cupboard Organisation: We empty and clean all cupboards, ensuring they are neat and ready for the next occupant.

Tailored to Property Managers and Tenants:Our service is designed to meet the strict standards required by landlords and property agencies, ensuring a hassle-free handover.

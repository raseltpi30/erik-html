General Cleaning Services

At Crystal Clean Sydney, our General Cleaning services are designed to maintain your space in top condition, ensuring it looks its best at all times. This service is ideal for regular upkeep of both homes, providing a clean and inviting environment for everyone.

What We Offer:

  • Floor Maintenance: Comprehensive sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping of all flooring types to remove dust and dirt.
  • Dusting: We thoroughly dust all surfaces, including hard-to-reach areas like ceiling fans, light fixtures, and window treatments to maintain a dust-free environment.
  • Baseboards & Windows: Cleaning of all baseboards and window sills to remove any accumulated dirt and grime.
  • Sanitisation: High-touch areas such as switch plates and doorknobs are sanitised and disinfected to prevent the spread of germs.
  • Tidying Up: We tidy up common areas, including making beds and organising magazines or decor to enhance the overall cleanliness.
  • Cobweb Removal: Our team ensures that all corners of your space are free from cobwebs, maintaining a neat appearance.

Customisable Frequency: Choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleaning visits according to your convenience and needs. Our flexible scheduling allows you to enjoy a consistently clean environment without any hassle.
